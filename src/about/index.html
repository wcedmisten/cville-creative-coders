About

C’ville Creative Coders was created by William Edmisten in 2023.

It was inspired by the final Strange Loop conference in St. Louis, which featured talks on all sorts of technical projects. From academia to industry, people came to share what they were passionate about. C’ville Creative Coders hopes to bring that same energy to Charlottesville! It's a place to share your passion, even if it's not yet ready for a bigger audience.
